SERVICES

Quality Labour Hire Ltd provides General Labour/Crew and Skilled Crew for events all over Auckland and around the Auckland region. Our commitment to training and safety ensures your events are staffed with competent, enthusiastic and quick acting staff who deliver beyond expectations. All of our crew undergo rigorous inductions and training to ensure complete compliance with New Zealand health and safety regulations.

​We offer Skilled Crew for the following purposes:

  • Marquee setup and Stage builders - our experts erect and take down marquees efficiently with zero delays.
  • Crew Chiefs - specialised in Live Events Supervising, Emergency First Aid and Health and Safety.
  • Forklift/Cherry picker operators - Can provide crew that are fully qualified and can operate all sizes of machine.
  • Runners, Valets and Qualified Drivers - conscientious, punctual, proactive and fully licenced for all categories of driving.
